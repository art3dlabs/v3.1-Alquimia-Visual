================================================================================
ANÁLISIS DE ESTILOS FOTOGRÁFICOS EXTRAÍDOS
================================================================================
Este archivo contiene una lista curada de estilos visuales detectados en la aplicación.
Cada estilo ha sido normalizado a un "Mid Shot" (Plano Medio) para facilitar la comparación.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESTILO 1: Cinematic Muted Realism
(Basado en: Cinematic Mirror Selfie / Cinematic Portrais)
PROMPT:
Mid shot of a person facing the camera with a calm expression. The setting is a modern interior with soft, natural light. Lighting is soft and diffused, creating creamy highlights and lifelike skin textures. Colors are a muted neutral palette. Aesthetic is ultra 4K cinematic realism, Kodak Portra 800 film tone.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESTILO 2: Cool Digital Glow
(Basado en: Tech Visionary / Sci-Fi / Gamer)
PROMPT:
Mid shot of a person looking intelligently at the camera. The setting is a dark server room or tech environment. Lighting consists of a strong backlight from blue/white LED lights and a cool, diffuse overhead light, casting a digital glow on the face. Dominant colors are cool blues, cyan, and deep blacks with medium contrast. Smooth metallic surfaces, zero grain.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESTILO 3: Gritty Backstage Contrast
(Basado en: Musician Backstage / Boxer V2)
PROMPT:
Mid shot of a person with an intense expression. The setting is a dark, rustic backstage area or gym. Lighting is an extreme and dramatic backlight coming from a bright source behind, outlining the features with a rim of white light. Dominant colors are deep blacks and high contrast beams. Authentic documentary look with visible grain for a raw feel.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESTILO 4: Earthy Studio Daylight
(Basado en: Artist Studio / Workshop)
PROMPT:
Mid shot of a person focused or looking at the camera. The setting is a spacious, dusty loft or workshop. Lighting is a strong, natural backlight beaming through a large industrial window, creating a dusty, dramatic halo around the subject. Dominant colors are earthy tones, ochres, and grays. Rich, realistic textures with light grain.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESTILO 5: Cosmic Purple Haze
(Basado en: Scientist Visionary / Galaxy themes)
PROMPT:
Mid shot of a person with an expression of awe. The setting is a dark environment illuminated by a large projection or cosmic light source. Lighting is a dramatic front light bathing the face in vibrant, ethereal colors like purples, blues, and magentas. Dominant colors are cool and cosmic with medium-high contrast. Finely detailed textures.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESTILO 6: Inferno Orange Glow
(Basado en: Master Pitmaster / Firefighter)
PROMPT:
Mid shot of a person sweating and looking intense. The setting is a rustic outdoor space at night. Lighting is dominated by an intense orange and red glow from a fire or embers acting as a powerful backlight and dramatic key light. Dominant colors are oranges, reds, and deep blacks with extreme contrast. Visceral grain and raw textures.

ESTILO 18: Vintage Analog Warmth
(Basado en: Polaroid / 70s Fan Photos)
PROMPT:

Dominant colors have a warm/yellow color cast, slightly faded shadows, and visible film grain. No white border, just the aesthetic of a vintage photo.
